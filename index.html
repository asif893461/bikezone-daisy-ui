Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” it. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Check the manufacturing date inside your helmet. Replace it if it’s older than 5 years or shows signs of damage or wear.

My bike has been in storage — is it safe to ride?

Inspect tires, brakes, and chains before riding. Lubricate the chain and ensure all bolts are tight for a safe ride.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

Follow local traffic laws, signal when turning, wear visible clothing, and always wear a helmet for safety.
